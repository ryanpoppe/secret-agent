You've identified the most critical asset in healthcare security: PHI Patient Data

Protected Health Information (PHI) is the crown jewel of healthcare data—and the primary target for cybercriminals. PHI includes any individually identifiable health information: medical records, treatment history, billing information, insurance details, even appointment schedules.

When PHI is compromised, the consequences are severe:

  • Regulatory penalties: HIPAA violations can cost millions in fines
  • Legal liability: Class-action lawsuits from affected patients
  • Reputational damage: Loss of patient trust and confidence
  • Identity theft: Stolen PHI sells for 50x more than credit card data on the dark web

This is why securing PHI throughout the document lifecycle is non-negotiable. From the moment a patient form is scanned to the final storage in your system, every step must be protected.

Vasion's solutions address PHI security at multiple levels: encrypted transmission, secure release printing (requiring physical authentication), automated redaction, compliant eSignatures, and audit trails that document every access.
