OUTPUT RULES ROUTING

Automated Logic for Mission-Critical Documents

Imagine this scenario: A hospital pharmacy printer fails during the night shift. Prescription labels need to print for morning medication rounds. Without intelligent routing, those documents are lost, patient care is delayed, and staff scramble to manually reroute jobs.

Output Rules Routing prevents this chaos by applying automated logic to handle print failures intelligently:

  • Automatic failover: When the primary printer fails, jobs instantly reroute to backup devices
  • Rules-based logic: Define routing priorities based on document type, department, time of day, or user role
  • Load balancing: Distribute print jobs across multiple devices to prevent bottlenecks
  • Geographic routing: Send jobs to the nearest available printer for faster pickup
  • Business continuity: Ensure mission-critical ERP and EMR documents always reach their destination

The system continuously monitors printer status and applies rules-and-routing intelligence to ensure business operations never stop due to hardware failure. This is especially critical in healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, and financial services where document output directly impacts operational effectiveness.

For example, Graham Healthcare Group uses Output Rules Routing to ensure patient records, lab results, and medication orders always print—even when primary devices are offline for maintenance or experiencing technical issues.
